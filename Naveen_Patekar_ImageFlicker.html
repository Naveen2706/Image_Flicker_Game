Image Flicker Game

Wrong-Flips : 0

Score : 0

Time-Out : 30 s

  1. Click any box after clicking it shows images
  2. Then click other box
  3. After that if the images are same in both boxes then you score is 10 for match
  4. But if they aren't match then your wrongflip is 1.
  5. If the two images aren't match you click on third box then previous boxes are hide
  6. If your score is greater or equal to 30 then win
  7. Only 16 wrongflips allowed after that your game is over.
